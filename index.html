Dio Brando is a fictional character who appeared in the Fantastic Adventures of JoJo, a Japanese manga series created and illustrated by Hirohiko Araki. He was the main villain of the first book in the series, Phantom Blood. He later served as the main villain of the third book in the series, stardust Crusader. He is a powerful vampire and a user of The Time stop station called The World.

Background: Dario Brando's poor son, an alcoholic who abused Dior's mother. Dior is too hostile to others. As the most prolific villain in the series, his defining characteristic is his unwavering ambition, which manifests itself in an unparalleled thirst for power, no matter what the cost.
